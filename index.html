VISION OF MY JOURNEY


Business Mission



My major purpose of starting is blog was my hunger for travelling. I really wanna
visit every corner of the world and experience there values , beliefs , the way
they think and everything. I wanna live with them and know them. I started this
journey in 2015 with my Instagram page “THEVOGUEFOODY”, where I started posting initially.
All the amazing and delicious dishes which I had around. By time different cafes and
restaurants offered me to come to there places and post about their brand on my
platform. There I realised I achieved first milestone of my journey. And there is
no stopping back, I wanna travel now!
